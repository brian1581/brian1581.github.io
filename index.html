Here's a little story I like to tell

Most people live their entire life within 150 miles of where they grew up. To each his/her own, but that just would and could not be me. For a long time I wanted to move from Minnesota to the West Coast. I decided to take two trips in 2011--Vancouver, B.C. to San Diego--and 2012--Phoenix, Vegas the Black Hills and Colorado. I did it to find the best fit for me. It's definitely the best decision I ever made.

I settled on Portland as the Pacific Northwest was spectacular, and I did have one friend and also a family member here. I decided to give living here two years before I committed to moving back home or staying put. It was a long, occasionally stressful yet memorable 25 hour drive from St. Paul, MN to Portland.
